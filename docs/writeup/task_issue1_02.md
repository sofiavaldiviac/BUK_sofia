## Motivation

The literature review needs to establish two parallel streams — monopsony × gender and gender × career dynamics — and show that their intersection is unexplored. This positions the paper's contribution clearly.

## Specific goal

Write a 2–3 page literature review organized around:
1. **Monopsony and gender wages** — Webber (2016), Sharma (2023), Caldwell & Danieli (2022): market power depresses women's wages, outside options shape within-firm outcomes. Gap: only studied for wages, not hiring/promotion.
2. **Gender and career dynamics** — Benson et al. (2026), Bertrand & Mullainathan (2004): promotion gaps persist after controlling for performance; hiring discrimination via audits. Gap: no connection to market structure.
3. **The missing intersection** — whether market power distorts hiring screening or promotion decisions by gender. This is the paper's contribution.
4. **Methodological antecedents** — Burdett & Mortensen (1998), Gibbons & Waldman (1999), Postel-Vinay & Robin (2002) for the model; Azar, Marinescu & Steinbaum (2022) for HHI measurement from application data.

## Success criteria

- [ ] Each literature stream clearly summarized with key findings
- [ ] Explicit identification of what existing work does NOT do (the gap)
- [ ] Paper's contribution stated as bridging the two streams
- [ ] References are accurate and correctly attributed
